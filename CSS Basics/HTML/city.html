Welcome to Paris

Known as the “City of Lights,” Paris is a global hub for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine.

Parisian Culture

Parisian culture is deeply rooted in art, literature, and style. Museums like the Louvre and Musée d’Orsay display masterpieces from centuries past.

Famous Landmarks

From the Eiffel Tower to the Notre-Dame Cathedral, Paris is home to some of the most iconic landmarks in the world.

Cuisine & Cafés

French cuisine, especially in Paris, celebrates rich flavors and refined presentation. Enjoy croissants, crêpes, and coffee in classic sidewalk cafés.
